You do not need to complete any math problems in this task. Below, we ask you to guess your rank in Task 2 (Tournament).

Remember that in Task 2 (Tournament), we randomly put you in a group of four and you solved as many addition problems as you could within 2 minutes. Now, we want to know what you think your rank among the four person group was.

For this survey, 1 indicates you having the highest score and 4 indicates you having the lowest score in the group. If your guess is correct, you will earn an additional 2 ECU. In case of a tie, every answer that could be considered correct will be rewarded as correct. For example, if the top two participants correctly answered the same number of questions, both participants could guess either 1 or 2 and be rewarded for a correct answer.
